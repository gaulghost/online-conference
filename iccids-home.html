About the Conference

International Conference on Computational Intelligence and Data Science (ICCIDS-2021) is organised with the objective of bringing together scientists, professors, research scholars, students and industrial experts in the field of Data Science and its various Applications to a common forum. Overall, the conference will provide the researchers and attendees with prospects for national and international collaboration and networking. ICCIDS-2021 will be held at EWCE. All the accepted papers (after double blinded peer review) are published in Elsevier SSRN.
